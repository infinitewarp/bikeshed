Bikeshed Calculator

The bikeshed calculator shows you how much money you cost the company by bikeshedding a particular discussion. This takes a list of gross annual salaries (obscured by default so you don't expose your wages to others) and a length of time for the discussion.

These calculations assume that your salaries pay for 40 hours/week for 50 weeks/year and do not account for taxes or withholdings; they are meant to describe the cost to the company.
